Our Paisley Story

Founded in 2010, we’ve been the heartbeat of the local music scene for over a decade.

Paisley Sounds started with a simple mission: to make high-quality music education
accessible to everyone in Renfrewshire. From our humble beginnings in a small studio
near the Abbey, we’ve grown into a community of over 300 students.
